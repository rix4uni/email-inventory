## email-inventory

Email OSINT for public bug bounty programs, Email list updated regularly.

This repo collects emails from different sources like Google, DuckDuckGo, Bing, Yahoo, Yandex, Github, etc.
If you found email releated to you or your company and you don't want to show them publicly, Please Give a request to remove that email files contains your email, i will remove those email files immediately.
